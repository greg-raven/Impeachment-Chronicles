We need to make sure EVERYONE is aware of this. A week ago, the economy was set to expand by 2.3% this quarter. Now it's forecasted to retract by 2.8%. That's a massive decline --- and it CAN'T be blamed on Biden. Again, it all happened inside the last *week*.

> Being able to drop a quarterly U.S. GDP prediction by 5.1% from +2.3% to -2.8% in a single week is one of the most impressive economic developments in the history of the world.
